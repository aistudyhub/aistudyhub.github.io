Using ChatGPT to Enhance Your Study Sessions

ChatGPT can act as a personal tutor: ask it to explain difficult concepts and generate custom examples.

Use simple prompts like: “Explain the theory of relativity in 3 bullet points” or “Create 5 practice questions on organic chemistry.”

You can also ask ChatGPT to summarize lengthy articles, create flashcards, or plan out weekly study schedules.

Pair ChatGPT with Notion AI to store, organize, and review session outputs seamlessly.

With this setup, study becomes more interactive, efficient, and tailored to your personal learning style.
